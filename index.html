Core Services

Coal Hauling

EMCAF started diversifying into transportation as far back as 2008, operating two mechanical horses drawing hired trailers.

Hazardous Goods Transportation

A leading South African bulk liquid carrier of general chemicals, supported by a fleet designed for dedicated operations.

Passenger Transportation

A growing Mpumalanga transport service with a fleet ranging from mini taxis to midi taxis contracted to EMCAF Engineering.

Construction

The company expanded into construction in 2013, driven by a vision of job creation within disadvantaged communities.

Graphics Manufacturing

With a strong background in Mpumalanga, EMCAF specializes in signage, road signs, advertising boards and graphic designing.

Tautliners

Utilization of super linked taut liners, flat decks, drop sides and tankers to help serve a variety of customer needs.
